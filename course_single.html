About This Course

This is the most comprehensive, modern full-stack web development course on AfriDigital. Designed with the African developer in mind, you'll go from absolute beginner to job-ready professional.

You'll build 15+ real-world projects including an e-commerce platform, a social media dashboard, and a fintech payment tool — all of which can go straight into your portfolio. By the end, you'll have the skills to work as a frontend developer, backend developer, or full-stack engineer at any company.

Our curriculum is updated every quarter to keep up with industry demands. You'll also get lifetime access to the AfriDigital community where 8,000+ fellow learners and mentors are active daily.
